WHO WE ARE

The proud words spoken by our founder continue to be the bedrock of our restaurant model since 19xx. Game Day Grill seizes the opportunity to be different from other grills, through our superb menu and our top-notch entertainment for all ages. We seek to create a place where one can have good food and celebrate family and the community. Just as our founder had, grilling food for the important days with the important ones.


ABOUT US

We are a family-friendly grill and bar, specializing in the cuisine of the Deep South. From pool tables to an arcade to multiple 22 inch Televisions, perfect for any event, there is fun for all. Knowing the importance of community from our founder, we have special Sports-Day Events for every game, unlike anything any other grill restaurant could hope to provide.
